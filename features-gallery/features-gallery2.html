Step 1
Preparing the device
Fill the SeekGlo device with serum, invert it, and let it stand for 1–2 minutes to saturate the needles. Securely close the cap before use.
Step 2
Cleanse your face
In the meantime, gently cleanse your face to remove makeup and impurities – this will allow your skin to optimally absorb the serum.
Step 3
Start the treatment and use the remaining serum
Start in the center of your face and work outward. Repeat 2-3 times. Gently pat any remaining serum onto your face and neck and leave on overnight.
Step 4
Care after treatment & cleaning of the device
Afterward, apply a gentle moisturizer—redness usually subsides after 15–20 minutes. Clean the device thoroughly and allow it to air dry completely.
Step 5
Enjoy the results
Let the treatment work and enjoy radiant, smooth skin. If you have any questions, we're always here to help you get the most out of your SeekGlo treatment.
